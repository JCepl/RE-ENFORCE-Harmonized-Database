# Bark Beetle Disturbance (1985–2023)

**Source:**  
European Forest Disturbance Atlas

**URL:**  
https://zenodo.org/records/13333034

**Licence:**  
Copernicus Data and Information Policy – open and free to use with attribution

**Citation:**  
Senf, C., et al. (2024). *European Forest Disturbance Atlas (1985–2023).*  
Zenodo. Available at: https://zenodo.org/records/13333034

**Original projection:**  
WGS 84 geographic coordinates (EPSG: 4326)

**Original resolution:**  
Annual disturbance-detection products at ~30 m resolution

**Processing:**  
- Annual disturbance-severity layers (1985–2023) extracted from the European Forest Disturbance Atlas  
- Reprojected to WGS 84 (EPSG: 4326)  
- Resampled to 30 arc-seconds (~1 km) using average aggregation  
- Compiled into a single harmonised raster covering Europe

**Use:**  
Provides a spatially explicit indicator of historical bark beetle disturbance for training, testing, and validation of disturbance-risk and forest-dynamics models across Europe.

**Form:**  
GeoTIFF stack

**Size:**  
6.11 MB

---

## Notes

Values represent relative disturbance intensity per year, aggregated across the **1985–2023** period.
