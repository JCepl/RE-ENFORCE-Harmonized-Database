EU-DEM Derived

Copernicus Land Monitoring Service / European Environment Agency (EEA) – EU-DEM v1.1

Available at: https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/web/gisco/geodata/digital-elevation-model/eu-dem

**Original projection:**  
ETRS89 / LAEA Europe (EPSG: 3035)

**Original resolution:**  
1-arc-second (~25 m)

**Processing:**  
- Reprojected to WGS 84 (EPSG: 4326)  
- Resampled to 30 arc-seconds (~1 km)

**Use:**  
Provides a slope for climatic, hydrological, and disturbance-risk modelling across Central Europe. Raster dataset representing the slope steepness of the terrain, derived from the EU-DEM elevation model (Copernicus).
Slope indicates the rate of elevation change across the surface, expressed as angular degrees from horizontal.
