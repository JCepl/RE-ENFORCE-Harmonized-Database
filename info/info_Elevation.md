**Original projection:**  
ETRS89 / LAEA Europe (EPSG: 3035)

**Original resolution:**  
1-arc-second (~25 m)

**Processing:**  
- Reprojected to WGS 84 (EPSG: 4326)  
- Resampled to 30 arc-seconds (~1 km)

**Use:**  
Provides harmonised elevation for climatic, hydrological, and disturbance-risk modelling across Central Europe.
-  Derived terrain attributes:
-  **Elevation** – mean elevation per 1 km cell 

**Form:**  
The GeoTIFF raster for Elevation.

**Size:**  
105.38 MB

## Notes

Description
Raster dataset representing terrain elevation (meters above sea level) derived from the EU-DEM dataset provided by Copernicus.
Tiles were downloaded from Eurostat GISCO, stitched together, and cropped to the defined European extent 
to provide a continuous elevation surface for the RE-ENFORCE domain.
